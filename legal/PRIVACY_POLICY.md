# SongScribe Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 10, 2026**

## Introduction

SongScribe ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile and web application ("the App").

## Data We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

SongScribe is designed as an **offline-first application**. The following data is stored locally on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers:

- **Songs and Lyrics**: All song content, chord annotations, and metadata you create
- **Audio Recordings**: All recordings you make within the app
- **App Settings**: Your preferences, theme choices, and configuration
- **Setlists**: Your setlist organization (Pro feature)

**You have full control over this data.** It remains on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

### Data We May Collect

When you use certain features, we may collect limited data:

#### Purchase Information
- Transaction records for in-app purchases (processed by Apple)
- Subscription status (to provide appropriate features)

#### Analytics Data (Optional)
If you opt in to analytics, we may collect:
- App usage patterns (which features are used)
- Device type and operating system version
- App crash reports and performance data
- General geographic region (country level only)

This data is collected in aggregate and cannot identify you personally.

#### Feedback and Support
If you contact us for support or submit feedback:
- Email address (if provided)
- Device and system information (if you consent)
- Description of issues or suggestions

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- **We do not access your song lyrics or content**
- **We do not access your audio recordings**
- **We do not sell or share your data with third parties for advertising**
- **We do not track your location**
- **We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files**

## How We Use Your Data

We use collected data only for:

1. **Providing the Service**: Managing subscriptions and entitlements
2. **Improving the App**: Understanding usage patterns to improve features
3. **Customer Support**: Responding to your inquiries and resolving issues
4. **Security**: Detecting and preventing fraud or abuse

## Third-Party Services

### Apple App Store
Your purchases are processed through Apple. Apple's privacy policy applies to transaction data: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

### Analytics (PostHog)
If analytics is enabled, we use PostHog for aggregated usage analytics. PostHog's privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy

### Error Monitoring (Sentry)
We use Sentry to collect crash reports and error data to help us fix bugs and improve app stability. Sentry may collect:
- Error messages and stack traces
- Device type and operating system
- App version and state at time of crash

This data is used solely for debugging and does not include your personal content (songs, lyrics, recordings). Sentry's privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

## Data Retention

- **Local Data**: Stored on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **Purchase Records**: Retained as required for legal and accounting purposes
- **Analytics Data**: Retained for up to 12 months in aggregate form
- **Support Communications**: Retained for up to 24 months

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

### Access and Export
- Export your songs and recordings at any time using the app's export features
- Request a copy of any data we hold about you

### Delete
- Delete any song, recording, or data within the app
- Request deletion of any data we hold about you
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data

### Opt Out
- Disable analytics in the app settings
- Opt out of marketing communications

### Data Portability
- Export your songs in standard formats (text, PDF)
- Export recordings as audio files

## Children's Privacy

SongScribe is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

- All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS)
- Local data is stored using secure device storage mechanisms
- We regularly review and update our security practices

## International Users

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your data may be processed in the United States where our servers are located. By using the App, you consent to such processing.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date above

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA:

- **Right to Know**: Request information about data collection practices
- **Right to Delete**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
- **Non-Discrimination**: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights
