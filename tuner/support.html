Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

Q: How do I grant microphone permission?

A: When you first open the app, iOS will prompt you to allow microphone access. If you denied it:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Scroll to "SongScribe Tuner"
  3. Enable "Microphone"
  4. Restart the app

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Yes! SongScribe Tuner is fully offline after download. No internet connection required.

Q: What instruments are supported?

A: All instruments! Choose from:

  • Chromatic: Any note (55-1200 Hz)
  • Guitar: Standard 6-string guitar tuning
  • Ukulele: Standard ukulele tuning
  • Bass: 4-string bass guitar
  • Violin: Standard violin tuning

Tuning Tips

Q: Why is the tuner not detecting my instrument?

A: Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. ‚úÖ Ensure microphone permission is granted
  2. ‚úÖ Play notes clearly and loudly
  3. ‚úÖ Reduce background noise (turn off music, TV, etc.)
  4. ‚úÖ Move closer to your device's microphone
  5. ‚úÖ Pluck/bow strings one at a time
  6. ‚úÖ Check that your device's microphone is working (try Voice Memos app)

Q: How accurate is the tuner?

A: SongScribe Tuner is accurate to within ¬±1 cent (1/100th of a semitone), comparable to professional hardware tuners. Accuracy depends on:

  • Microphone quality
  • Background noise levels
  • Instrument volume and clarity

Q: What is the reference pitch (A4)?

A: The reference pitch is the frequency of the A above middle C (A4). Standard tuning uses 440 Hz, but some orchestras and historical instruments use different pitches (430-450 Hz). You can customize this in Settings.

Settings & Customization

Q: How do I change the reference pitch?

A:

  1. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  2. Tap "Reference Pitch"
  3. Adjust the slider (430-450 Hz)
  4. Default is 440 Hz (standard tuning)

Q: What's the difference between instrument presets?

A: Each preset optimizes the frequency range for specific instruments:

  • Chromatic: Widest range, all notes
  • Guitar: Optimized for E2-E4 (standard tuning)
  • Ukulele: Optimized for G4-A4 (soprano ukulele)
  • Bass: Optimized for E1-G2 (4-string bass)
  • Violin: Optimized for G3-E5 (standard tuning)

Choose the preset closest to your instrument for best results.

Q: Can I use dark mode?

A: Yes! Dark mode follows your iOS system settings. To enable:

  1. Open iOS Settings
  2. Tap Display & Brightness
  3. Select "Dark"
